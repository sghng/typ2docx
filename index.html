What happens to my data?

Your data will be sent to a web service container hosted on Render, in which runs the typ2docx tool, which relies on sending the compiled PDF of your project to Adobe PDF Services for conversion.

Your files are not stored by this tool and are deleted after conversion. The source code for the converter, this website, and the web service are available on GitHub.
